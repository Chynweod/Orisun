WHO ARE WE?


Orisun School of Hospitality is a project of the Women's Board Educational Co-operation society. Women's board is a Nigerian, not-for-profit, non-governmental organization, created in 1972 to contribute to women's development in both rural and urban settings.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to educate the total woman, providing her with hospitality skills and personality development training for service to the family, industry and society at large


ORISUN SCHOOL TIMELINE

1982

Started at rented premises in Bodija Ibadan as Imoran Catering Department.

1990

Moved to permanent site and was approved by the Ministry of Education as Orisun school of Catering.

1992

For the first time students were presented for the City & Guilds London Examination in basic cookery and home craft.

1994

The West African Examination Council ( WAEC ) accredited Orisun school of catering as an instruction and examination center for WAEC 894 technical examinations in Hotel and catering.

1995

The National Business and Technical Examinations Board ( NABTEB ) accorded the Center recognition as an instruction and examination center for the National Technical Examinations ( NTC )

2015

Orisun School of Catering clocked 25 years in empowering Nigerian women and young girls with the professional approach to hospitality services and in the running of homes. The ministry of education gave approval for a change of name to Orisun School of Hospitality.

2018

The Institute for Tourism Professionals ( ITP ) also also accorded Orisun school recognition as an approved training center for the Nigerian Skills Qualification ( NSQ )

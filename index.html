Academic Information


Calendar

Each academic session is made up of two semesters and each semester is made up of 15 - 18 weeks ( inclusive of examination periods )


Entry qualification

At least three credit level passes in WASCE / SSCE / NECO and a good command of the english language


Application forms are available at the school for #1000.


The NSQ system

From the begining of the semester the school will begin making use of the NSQ system in both teaching and assessment. NSQs are work based competency oriented qualifications that are achieved by proving competency in a range of skills that make up a unit or certificate. Development of professional expertise is enhanced through the school's emphasis on efficient working habits, cost-saving methods, personal grooming and managment skills.
